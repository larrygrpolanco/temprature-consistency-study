[1a] For coral reefs, three major stressors have been identified as playing important roles in determining coral reef structure and ecosystem functioning: climate change, nutrient loading/water pollution, and overfishing, and these stressors have been studied extensively in isolation (NorstrÃ¶m and others 2016; Harborne and others 2017; Hughes and others 2017).
